About Us

10+ Years Experience In Solar & Renewable Energy Industry

PT. Prima Energi Nusasurya is a company engaged in the field of renewable energy, specifically solar power plants. (Pembangkit Listrik Tenaga Surya). This company was established in 2024. Our headquarters are located at Jalan Villa Dago Tol 15414 Ciputat Banten No 6. Our company focuses on renewable energy, with our core business centered on the distribution, installation, maintenance, and services of Photovoltaic Systems.
